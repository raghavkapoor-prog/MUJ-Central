✍️Some Bonus Tips

There is no escape 🏃‍♂️ route when it comes to exams. But there is a way to tackle them efficiently and effectively. From arriving early, to attempting the easy questions first, this article has some great exam preparation tips 💡.

Exam season can be a stressful 🤯 time. Pre-exam anxiety can easily affect even the best students. Look through these handy examination tips to help you through this difficult time.

🌱 Take a reality check of your grey areas Analyse and review your performance of the previous tests and develop test taking strategies. Note down the points and areas where you need to improve and focus on in the final exams.

🌱 Arrive well on time Reaching about 15 to 20 minutes before gives you time for mental preparation for the exams. It relaxes you to focus on the task on hand. Positive thoughts that you think can have a calming effect on you.

🌱 Stay relaxed and confident Make yourself comfortable and have enough work space. Sit with an erect spine. Remind yourself that you are well prepared and are going to do well. If you find yourself anxious – take several slow, deep breaths to relax.

🌱 Read directions carefully Read each and every question in detail and visualize how you plan to write the answer. If there is time, quickly look through the paper for an overview. Note key terms, jot down brief points and make comments that come to your mind.

🌱 Answer questions in a strategic order Answer easy questions first. Next answer difficult questions or those with the highest marks.

🌱 Review your answers Resist the temptations of leaving the hall before time. Review to make sure that you have answered all questions, have not made silly mistakes, numbered answers correctly, punctuations and decimal points.

Lastly, after reading the above study tips for students – highlight or make a mental note of what appealed to you most. Note it and follow it to get the best out of yourself.
